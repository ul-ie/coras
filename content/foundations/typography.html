Our typography plays an important role in ensuring our digital communication is accessible, optimised for readability and reinforces our brand.

Fonts

We use three font families and a range of weights, detailed below.

Families

Our three font families are Inter, Saol and Formula Condensed.

Name Type CSS Class Usage
Inter variable font-sans Inter is our sans-serif font used by default on all elements, including headings.
Saol static font-serif Saol is our serif font which we use selectively for headings and quotes.
Formula Condensed static font-display Formula Condensed is our display font selectively used for headings.

Headings

We use heading levels from h1 down to h4 and discourage use of anything more granular than that. The heading font sizes are set in rem units, and related properties like margin-top and margin-bottom are set in em units, to stay proportionate to the headings.

A preview of the heading sizes are below, including their margins (shaded area) and their initial (mobile) size and their maximum (desktop) size.

Abbreviations
